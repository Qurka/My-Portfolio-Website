About Me

Hello World, my name is Raphael Cannavacciuolo I am 21 years old and based in The Netherlands, The Hague. At the moment I am studying at The Hague university. Following Communication and Multimedia Design. I would describe my self as a calm person that can keep his head cool, people always tell me I dont know stress. Sinds I can remeber I have always been bussy with creating and making things. People say I am a positive thinker and look at things from the bright side. Also in my spare time I like to follow the newest tech news and learn about new technologies.

Backstory When I was younger I was always busy building and creating things, I played a lot with legos also when was a bit older I was always crafting wooden swords, spears, bows, shields and play crazy battles in the woods with my friends. When I got older I got more interested in technologies. When, I was around the age of 11 I played a lot of videos games and also used to make my own gameplay videos and edit them into cool compilations. This is also where my interested in different technologies mostly came from. When I was only 13 years old I had built my own Computer with no experience just some YouTube videos. Because I played a lot of video games I got really interested in how video games were made.

I really liked all the tech stuff from CGI Videos, to 3D modeling and Game engines. I got interested in developing and coding games but at this point I was more into the art stuff especially 3D technologies. I then went to an art school called Grafisch Lyceum Rotterdam to learn everything about Graphic Design and Game Art. The coding scene didn't really get my interest until I found out about blockchain technologies from a friend in art school. I was really fascinated by this technologie and the potentials the technologie could bring. So I started to learn everything about it, and got interested in how they build and code these blockchains. In the process of learning about blockchain I found out about cryptocurrencies. I was reading about all these coins on websites, most of these websites were really modern and nicely designed, that's where my interested started for web design and how I got into UIX and Front-end design.
