OVERVIEW

About the project

Travel Further is a backpacking app focused on backpackers. To help out these backpackers, we game up with the solution of an centralized app. Which holds all the necessary information a traveler needs of the countries and all the cities. The app will also recommend good hostels, restaurants, events and activities based on the travelers interest and location. The traveler can book these things through the app.

The app also offers some social aspects, to bring the backpackers together and create a community. like finding other backpackers that are close by. The option to attend and host events. The option to look for an travel buddy to adventure with and a lot more to come. People can also share their adventures by writing blogs which other travelers can follow.
