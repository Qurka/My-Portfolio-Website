OVERVIEW

About the project

I got the assignment to design a donation page for the Koios platform. The mission of KOIOS is to realize a blockchain-based educational infrastructure, which is openly accessible to everyone in the world. An "open learning infrastructure", where "global learning communities" openly share, learn, earn and interact can get in touch.

KOIOS is currently missing a piece of goodwill and would like to do something for charities and create more affinity. They also want to test whether donating has a positive impact on learning (via incentives such as status e.g. by receiving NFTs/Badges).

My role:

UI-UX Design

Tools:

Pen & paper, Adobe XD

PROCESS

First I started with some research. With all the information from the research process I started developing some sketches, when I had the concept I was looking for I started creating the wireframes. Then finally completed a Hi-Fi prototype of the design.
